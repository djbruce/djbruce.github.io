Broadly I am interested in commutative algebra and algebraic geometry, as well as their interactions with other fields like number theory and topology. My specific research interests are a bit widespread, but currently I spend most of my time thinking about free resolutions, syzygies, and the geometry of the Hilbert scheme of points.
